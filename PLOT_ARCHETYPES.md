# Plot Archetypes - Simple Explanations

These questions help the game guess your film by its main story type.

## 1. **Romantic Fugitive** 🏃‍♂️💕
**Question:** "Do the lovers run away together from family?"

**Meaning:** A boy and girl fall in love but their families don't accept it. So they run away together. The whole movie is about them escaping and trying to be together.

**Example:** A poor boy loves a rich girl. They run away from her angry father.

---

## 2. **Lookalike Twin** 👥
**Question:** "Does the hero use a twin or double for tricks?"

**Meaning:** The hero has a twin brother or looks exactly like someone else. He uses this double identity to confuse bad guys or play tricks.

**Example:** Good twin is a police officer, bad twin is a criminal. They keep confusing each other.

---

## 3. **Cop Protagonist** 👮
**Question:** "Is the hero a police officer?"

**Meaning:** The main character works as a cop trying to catch bad guys and fight crime.

**Example:** A police officer fights against a crime boss in the city.

---

## 4. **Frontier Western** 🤠🐴
**Question:** "Are there horses, cowboys, or desert/western setting?"

**Meaning:** The movie looks like an old American Western movie. Has horses, deserts, bounty hunters, and gunfights.

**Example:** A lone hero on a horse rides through the desert fighting bandits.

---

## 5. **Neighborhood Brotherhood** 👥🤝
**Question:** "Is it about street friends fighting together?"

**Meaning:** A group of friends from the same neighborhood stick together. They fight against crime or help each other survive in dangerous streets.

**Example:** Four childhood friends from a poor neighborhood become heroes to protect their street.

---

## 6. **Faction Feud** ⚔️
**Question:** "Is there a long family revenge or land war?"

**Meaning:** Two big families or groups have been fighting each other for many years. The hero fights to end this war or get revenge.

**Example:** Two farming families fight for 20 years over who owns the land. The hero tries to stop it.

---

## 7. **Divine Intervention** ✨🪄
**Question:** "Are there gods, magic, or mythical creatures?"

**Meaning:** The movie has magic, gods from Hindu stories, or supernatural powers. Fantasy elements mix with action.

**Example:** A hero gets magical powers from a goddess and fights demons from another world.

---

## How the Game Uses These

When the game knows:
- **Which actor** (Chiranjeevi, Balakrishna, etc.)
- **Which director** (K. Raghavendra Rao, etc.)

It then asks about these **story types** to pick the exact movie. Because even though one director made 8 films with the same actor, each film has a different story type!
